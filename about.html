Who We Are

Metsimantsi is a pioneering beverage company based in Kathu & Kuruman, specializing in the purification and sale of bottled water, ice cubes, and fresh fruit juices. As a women-driven enterprise, we combine innovation with tradition to deliver exceptional products that meet the highest standards of quality and hygiene.

Our commitment extends beyond business - we're dedicated to community upliftment, job creation, and empowering youth through practical skills and employment opportunities.

Our Journey

From vision to reality - The Metsimantsi story

The Vision

Prudence Boipelo Mahlangu identified the need for quality, locally-produced beverages in the Northern Cape region. The vision was clear: provide clean hydration while creating jobs.

Company Formation

Boipelo Jwa Rona (Pty) Ltd was officially registered on 06 September 2024, marking the beginning of our formal business operations.

First Production

Our first batch of purified bottled water was produced and distributed, receiving positive feedback from the local community.

Product Expansion

We expanded our offerings to include ice cube production, fresh fruit juices, and custom label printing services.

BBBEE Certification

Achieved Level 1 BBBEE Contributor status, enhancing our procurement recognition to 135%.

Growing Impact

Today, Metsimantsi serves both individual and business clients across Kathu and Kuruman, promoting healthy living and local economic development.

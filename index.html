About Boipelo Jwa Rona

A women-driven business committed to quality, hygiene, and community empowerment in the Northern Cape region.

Our Mission

To provide clean, refreshing, and safe beverages while delivering innovative labeling solutions that promote quality, health, and local business branding.

Our Vision

To become a leading regional provider of purified water and custom-labeled beverage solutions, recognized for quality, affordability, and community empowerment.

Who We Are

Boipelo Jwa Rona, trading as Metsimantsi, is a dynamic women-driven business based in Kathu & Kuruman, specializing in purified bottled water, ice cubes, fresh juices, and custom label printing.

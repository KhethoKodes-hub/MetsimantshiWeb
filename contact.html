Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Saturday deliveries are available from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. For large orders or special requests, please contact us in advance to arrange suitable delivery times.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer competitive bulk discounts for businesses, events, and regular orders. Discounts start at 10% for orders over R1,000 and can go up to 25% for large recurring orders. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by calling us at 079 036 9956, sending an email to Prudencekolberg23@gmail.com, or by filling out the contact form on this page. For first-time orders, we recommend calling to discuss your specific needs.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, EFT (electronic funds transfer), and credit/debit cards. For corporate clients, we offer 30-day payment terms upon credit approval. All payments are processed securely.

What is your return policy?

If you receive damaged or defective products, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a replacement or refund. We stand by the quality of our products and will make it right if you're not satisfied.

How long does custom label printing take?

Standard label printing takes 3-5 business days from design approval. Rush orders (24-48 hours) are available for an additional fee. The timeline depends on the complexity of the design and the quantity ordered.
